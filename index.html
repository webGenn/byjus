BYJU’S Tuition Centres (BTCs)

BYJU’S Tuition Centres is an initiative of BYJU’S to propel quality education at affordable rates via easy accessibility. We have set up many BYJU’S Tuition Centres in different cities across India. While our aim is to provide tuition classes that help students score well in academics. We also prioritise the development of critical thinking and concept-building. It prepares our students with excellent problem-solving abilities required in competitive exams conducted outside schools.


We offer tuition classes in two of the most important subjects at school - Maths and Science. Since these two subjects need strategic teaching methods to explain concepts and develop skills related to problem-solving, we use a lot of advanced teaching aids that use high-end technology to present explanations of concepts and examples more visually. This helps our students learn various concepts in Science and Maths by relating those to easily observable phenomena in their daily lives.

Prominent features of BYJU’S Tuition Centres (BTCs):

  • We offer Tuition classes for grades 4 to 10
  • Tuition classes are offered for students from CBSE, ICSE and other selected state boards across the country.
  • Offline classes can be easily accessed by students living within a radius of 5 kilometres around our tuition centres.
  • Apart from school assessments, we also prepare students for other kinds of competitive exams.

Unique Features of BYJU’S Tuition Centres:

Hybrid Classes

Our tuition centres offer classes in a hybrid mode. Students can access both online and offline classes according to their convenience. This provides relaxation in students’ daily schedules - making them more energetic and enthusiastic about learning.

Two- teacher Model in Online Classes

Our online classes have a two-teacher model, which means that after a complete session on explanations and concept building, we provide a second session where there is another teacher to address your child's doubts and queries. If they still have doubts/queries after this session, they can always resolve those at our offline classes at their registered BYJU’S Tuition Centres (BTCs).

Track Record of Performance for Analysis

At BYJU’S Tuition Centres, tutors are constantly testing, analysing and assessing your child's progress. This information is passed on to parents accordingly with suggestions and remedial measures for regular improvement.

Personalised Attention to Students

We are all for a healthy learning environment. Teachers at BTCs maintain cordial relationships with students so that there is no gap in communication from their end. Furthermore, each student gets personalised attention from teachers who help them bridge learning gaps and learn more than what is merely given in the syllabus.


BYJU’S Tuition Centres deliver tuition classes in a hybrid mode. With our teachers' dedicated attention towards students, learning has become more exciting and encouraging than ever. BYJU’S aims to revolutionise the teaching of Maths and Science. We want to reach out to every child to prepare them for the success they have always dreamed of.


Join us in this journey of success and enlightenment to help every child discover their true potential.
